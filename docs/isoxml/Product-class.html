Product

Product class

An element that describes a single product. A Product can be related to a ProductGroup.

To determine which products belong to a specific ProductGroup, the groupIdRef values of all products shall be examined for a match with a particular ProductGroup.id value. Products refer both to products being used in a task (i.e. crop protection chemicals) and products being produced by a task (agricultural products).
